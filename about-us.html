Redleg Company- Technology and Program Management

Redleg Company is founded and managed by John King. John has a diverse background in private and government organizations. For 15 years, John worked in a variety of specialist and managerial roles both in the technical and retail space in the financial services industry. John won a number of accolades as a customer service specialist, FINTECH product specialist and later a technology consultant driving field user adoption for a Fortune 100 company. Most recently John worked a number of years with various esteemed customers in the United States Special Operations Command performing mission and logistic support, relationship management and consulting related to technical and tactical unmanned aerial systems services. John currently holds a TS/SCI DoD security clearance.

John also holds the rank of Captain in the United States Army Reserve. For over 25 years, John has held various specialist and leadership positions related to multi-modal logistics, precision Fires support and Civil Affairs both in overseas contingent operations and training in the US. Redleg Company has a combined total of 28 years of experience in the Financial Services industry included branch and project management. Redleg Company can also assist in online marketing through various social media platforms.
